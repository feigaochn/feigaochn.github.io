IFTTT is Testing Sina Weibo Channel

Published: Sat 28 June 2014
By Fei Gao

In posts.

tags: ifttt weibo

This morning, I received the invitation to join the Sina Weibo preview on ifttt.

The Channel is in its early stages of development. Currently it supports two triggers and one action. triggers: * new post by you * new post by you with hashtag action: * publish a new post

The triggers support the following ingredients, where the last one is for with hashtag only. Text: The weibo status text. FirstLinkUrl: The first URL found in the weibo. UserName: The username of the person who posted the weibo status. PhotoURL: The URL to the attached photo, if present. WeiboURL: The URL to the weibo status. CreatedAt: Date and time the weibo was posted. * TextNoHashtag: The weibo status text without the specified hashtag.

The action (posting new post) has two fields: the text, limited to 140 characters; and an optional photo url.
